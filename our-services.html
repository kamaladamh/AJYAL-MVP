Our Process

How we process projects.

A five-step journey from Introduction and SolarCheck through bespoke design, engineering, production, installation, and maintenance.

01Introduction & SolarCheck

During an initial meeting, we map the project's wishes and show suitable color and design options. We analyze energy, orientation, optimal facade panel area, and potential CO2 savings.

02Bespoke Design & Colouring

We offer a tailor-made process for architects where we jointly develop a design that fits the project, including samples to achieve the right color and design.

03Engineering & SolarScan

We develop detailed insight into the application of solar panels through solar study, shadow fall, design options, and detailing.

04Production & Installation

We develop the solar design panels, select the correct mounting system, and prepare installation plans with building details.

05Maintenance & Support

We support the facade panels and system through maintenance, repair, and replacement needs.
